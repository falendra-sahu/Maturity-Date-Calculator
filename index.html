Post office RD

Current Interest Rate

From 01.01.2024: 6.7% per annum (quarterly compounded)

Account Opening

  • Minimum amount: ₹100/- per month
  • Additional deposits in multiples of ₹10/-
  • No maximum limit

Who Can Open?

  • Single adult
  • Joint Account (up to 3 adults)
  • Guardian on behalf of minor
  • Guardian on behalf of person of unsound mind
  • Minor above 10 years in own name
Note: Any number of accounts can be opened

Deposits & Defaults

  • Account can be opened by cash/cheque
  • Default fee: ₹1 per ₹100 denomination per month
  • Account becomes discontinued after 4 regular defaults
  • Revival possible within two months of 4th default

Advance Deposits & Loans

  • Advance deposits up to 5 years possible
  • Loan facility: Up to 50% of balance after 12 installments
  • Loan interest: 2% + RD interest rate

Maturity & Closure

  • Maturity period: 5 years (60 monthly deposits)
  • Premature closure possible after 3 years
  • Extension possible for another 5 years
